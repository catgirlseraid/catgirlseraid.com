About Me

I started editing OSM in June 2024 after finding out about it through a friend who saw me editing google maps and recommeneded i try contributing to OSM as users have greater control over their edits. At the begining i was focusing on smaller projects such as updating road alignment, but now i've moved into almost every aspect of OSM. It's very rewarding knowing the work i do is improving the lives of people, whether its roads in rural Ethopia or Footpaths and Crossings in Urban New Zealand.


I vary what i work on, from adding info from notes where possbile to micromapping universities. I also enjoy Maproulette challenges and data validation/fixing incorrect data. Please find a list of some of the major projects i've done below, as well as areas i've micromapped.
